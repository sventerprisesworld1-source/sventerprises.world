Our Professional Services

Complete sourcing, fabrication and mechanical solutions

Industrial Procurement

Genuine, on-time sourcing of automation, switchgear, safety, pneumatics, tools, and mechanical components from verified OEM networks.

  • Pan-India vendor network with brand authenticity
  • RFQ to dispatch workflow with documentation
  • Project kitting & BOM validation to reduce delays
  • QA checks, warranty & compliance support

We align supply with your production schedules using reliable logistics, pre-QA checks, and site-ready packaging.

Fabrication Works

Precision fabrication of frames, supports, skids and assemblies built to your drawings, tolerances and delivery schedules.

  • Custom metal structures, jigs and fixtures
  • CNC profile cutting, bending and fit-up accuracy
  • Dimensional inspections and weld certifications
  • Surface preparation and finish-ready delivery

From prototyping to repeat batches, we align tooling, QA and logistics so your assemblies reach the line ready for installation.

Welding Services

Professional MIG, TIG & ARC welding for industrial structures, machinery frames, and precision components with strong workmanship standards.

  • Structural & fabrication welding for MS / SS / Aluminum
  • Skilled welders with AWS/DIN standard practices
  • Fixture-based accuracy and distortion control
  • Optional NDT inspection (DPT, MPI, UT, RT)

Whether it's production welding or industrial repair, we follow WPS guidelines and ensure strong joint penetration, alignment, and zero-defect output.

Painting & Coating

Industrial-grade surface prep and coatings—primer, epoxy, PU and powder—tailored to environment and service life.

  • Surface prep (degreasing, blasting, sanding)
  • Coating system selection for site conditions
  • DFT checks and adhesion testing
  • Clean, consistent finish with curing control

We engineer coating systems for durability—balancing cost, application method and in-field performance.

Tools & Hardware Supply

Ready stock of hand tools, power tools, fasteners and workshop essentials—sourced from trusted OEMs and distributors.

  • Power tools, cutting & grinding consumables
  • Fasteners, anchors, clamps and fittings
  • Measuring & layout instruments
  • Brand options to meet budget & performance

We simplify tool procurement with quick quotes, verified brands and on-time dispatch across plants and sites.

Mechanical Assemblies

Assembly of custom mechanical kits and sub-systems with torque specs, fit checks and functional verification.

  • BOM-based assembly and kitting
  • Tolerance control and fastener torqueing
  • Labeling, packing and documentation
  • Pilot runs, feedback and repeatability

We deliver assemblies that integrate faster at site—reducing idle time and improving commissioning throughput.

Machinery Supply & Maintenance

Sourcing and sustaining essential shop-floor machinery with installation guidance, spares and scheduled maintenance.

  • Selection support for capacity, duty and footprint
  • Commissioning & handover documentation
  • Preventive maintenance plans & spares lists
  • On-call assistance to minimize downtime

We help you choose the right machines, keep them running reliably and extend life through parts and maintenance discipline.
